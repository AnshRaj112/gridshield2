1. Executive Context    
Lumina Energy operates a suburban Mumbai distribution zone.   
Under Maharashtras  Availability Based Tari  (ABT) regulations, Power Distribution Companies 
must submit a  2-day ahead load forecast at 15-minute resolution  to the State Load Dispatch 
Centre (SLDC).   
Deviation from submitted schedules attracts nancial penalties.   
Under ABT:   
 Under-forecasting (Actual > Forecast)  costly grid drawal   
 Over-forecasting (Forecast > Actual)  ine iciency + deviation charge   
 Penalties are asymmetric   
 Peak-hour deviations carry higher nancial and operational risk   
Forecast accuracy is no longer a statistical objective.   
It is a nancial exposure minimization problem under regulatory constraint .  
You are engaged as the  Forecast Risk Advisory Team .
